### A small doorway behind the posts

This page is the small doorway behind the posts. When a reader's opinion or question reaches this side, the writing stops being a private notebook and slowly grows into **a record of conversations**. Letting a sentence written in Nihonbashi travel to someone between Seoul and Tokyo and come back as a reply — that return trip is one of the quiet pleasures I count on keeping.

---

### What I welcome

- **Feedback on posts** — numbers, facts, awkward phrasing. I take all of it gratefully.
- **Topics you'd like to see covered** — urban investment in Tokyo, Korea–Japan macro issues, Nihonbashi neighbourhood notes, or personal essays; any of the four axes is fine.
- **Collaboration, guest writing, and media quotations** — interviews, newsletter contributions, or press references.
- **Just a hello** — a word from a reader who shares the same skyline is already enough.
