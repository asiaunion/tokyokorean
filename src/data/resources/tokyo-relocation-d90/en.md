## D-90 · D-60 · D-30 · D-7

This is **not legal or tax advice**. Use it as a question list before official sources and licensed professionals.

### D-90 — Choose your path

- [ ] Primary stay route: work, business manager, spouse, highly skilled, digital nomad, etc.
- [ ] Skim [Japan visa & permanent residence paths](/en/posts/japan-visa-paths-permanent-business-manager-asset-holders/)
- [ ] Budget: deposit, agency fees, 6 months living costs (two FX scenarios)
- [ ] Shortlist 2–3 wards for commute and rental market

### D-60 — Housing & paperwork

- [ ] Start rental search (guarantor / key money norms)
- [ ] Read [Tokyo moving & contract notes](/en/posts/tokyo-moving-contracts-two-notes/)
- [ ] Bank transfer and Japan account options
- [ ] Cross-border gift/inheritance basics: [KO–JP tax primer](/en/posts/korea-japan-inheritance-gift-tax-cross-border-basics/)

### D-30 — Execute

- [ ] Lease draft: restoration, renewal, termination clauses
- [ ] Corporate vs personal holding: [after-tax sketch](/en/posts/japan-corporate-vs-personal-rental-after-tax-sketch/)
- [ ] Utilities and ward office registration timeline

### D-7 — Departure

- [ ] Passport, visa docs (originals + copies)
- [ ] Align temporary stay vs move-in date
- [ ] Emergency contacts (home country + Japan)
